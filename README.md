**Haunted Zombie Rush** is a 3D endless jumper game built with Unity, inspired by the mechanics of Flappy Bird. Instead of a bird, you control a zombie navigating through a haunted world filled with moving pillars. Simple to play, hard to master ‚Äî how far can you get before it's game over?

---

## üéÆ Gameplay

- Tap or click to make the zombie "jump" or float upward.
- Avoid crashing into dynamically moving pillars.
- The game gets progressively harder the longer you survive.
- Score increases as you successfully pass obstacles.
